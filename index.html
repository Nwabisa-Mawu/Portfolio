About Me

Hi! I'm Nwabisa Mawu, a software developer who loves turning code into creative, functional applications. Over the past year, I’ve been building responsive, scalable web apps and mastering the fine art of SQL— because what's an adventure without some storage?
I see every project as an opportunity to learn, teach, and collaborate. I’m a quick learner, a dependable team player, and always up for some debugging—there's nothing quite like that "aha!" moment when it all clicks.
When I'm not solving coding challenges, you'll find me either immersed in a fantasy world where gravity doesn’t exist and I’m a telekinetic superhero, or elbow-deep in cookie dough—making my heart happy and my pancreas... a little concerned.
So whether you want to talk code, swap console horror stories, or brainstorm the next big thing, I’m your person. Let’s build something great together!
